Fabric Pamphlet: Children's Waiting Room

Children often experience anxieties when going to the doctor, especially when in the waiting room. With this project I looked to create a gentler more calm experience by reimagining objects that are in a waiting room but with more tactile materials such as fabric and felt.

Catalog Publication: Ryan Shorosky

Cataloging and changing Ryan Shorosky's photography so that it functions in a different way. In a sense taking apart each photograph to give it a new meaning. (A total of 100 pages, only a few are shown.)

Systems of Coronavirus

A graphic representation of how interconnected the political, economic, and environmental challenges of the coronavirus are. The aesthetic choice was made to mimic government propaganda from the 50's and 60's to emulate a sense of dystopia that harkens back to events like the atomic bombs and nuclear threats.
